Services

  • Accomodation


    Unitech Adventist Residential College (UARC) offers a welcoming accommodation experience for its students, designed to foster both comfort and academic success. The residential facilities are design to foster effective study, meticulously clean, and of exceptionally quality, ensuring that students have a conducive environment for study and personal growth. Each living space is thoughtfully equipped to support the diverse needs of students, promoting a harmonious blend of learning and community living.

  • Diversity


    One of the standout features of UARC is its commitment to inclusivity. Students from various Christian denominations are welcomed without discrimination, creating a vibrant and diverse community. This multicultural environment not only enriches the student experience but also emphasizes the college's core values of promoting Christian principles and values. Through shared experiences and communal activities, students are encouraged to grow in their faith while building lasting relationships with peers from different backgrounds.

  • Security Services


    Safety and security are paramount at UARC. The college collaborates with several reputable security service providers, including G4S, Uniforce, and its own UARC private security team, to ensure the safety and well-being of all residents. These security services are vigilant and proactive, providing 24/7 surveillance and support throughout the campus. Regular patrols, emergency response protocols, and well-trained security personnel create a secure environment where students can focus on their studies without unnecessary concerns for their safety.

    The combination of high-quality accommodations, a supportive and inclusive community, and robust security measures makes Unitech Adventist Residential College an exceptional choice for students seeking a safe and nurturing environment in which to thrive academically and spiritually.
